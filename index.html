What is Angels Theraphy?

Angels Therapy is a comprehensive and multidisciplinary approach to treating OMD that addresses the various symptoms and underlying causes of the disorder. Angels Therapy is an interventional treatment approach for Orofacial Myofunctional Disorder (OMD), which includes airway obstruction, nasal congestion, abnormal tongue posture, lip incompetence, abnormal eating habits, and swallowing abnormalities.

  • Wearing the C4 appliance and frequent chewing are two core treatments in Angel Therapy.
  • Intense nasal breathing during exercise with the C4 appliance can lead to nasal airway remodeling, resulting in improved airflow, increased mucociliary clearance, strengthening of respiratory muscles, and reduced nasal congestion.
  • Frequent chewing contributes to brain development by stimulating the brain, releasing neurotransmitters, developing motor skills, providing sensory integration, and strengthening jaw muscles and bones.
  • The therapy involves a multidisciplinary approach that includes assessment, addressing nasal breathing, glossal retraining, lip rehabilitation, improving eating and chewing habits, and swallowing retraining.

By addressing the various symptoms and underlying causes of OMD, Angels Therapy can improve orofacial function, speech, and overall quality of life for patients with OMD.

C4 Tooth Positioners Orthodontic Treatment

C4 tooth positioners are custom-made elastic appliances used in orthodontics to guide teeth into their ideal positions. With the integration of digital technology, orthodontists can now use digital software to design and fabricate more precise and efficient tooth positioners.

This has several benefits, including:

  • Enhanced precision in tooth movement analysis and planning
  • Improved patient outcomes with better prediction of final occlusion
  • Increased efficiency with streamlined design and fabrication processes

The use of C4 tooth positioners in orthodontic treatment has led to improved treatment outcomes and greater patient satisfaction. By utilizing digital technology to design tooth positioners according to optimal occlusion principles, orthodontists can achieve proper tooth position and angulation during early orthodontic treatment, ultimately leading to better results and a more comfortable experience for patients.

C4 Appliances

C4 hybrid combination device can treat obstructive sleep apnea (OSA) by holding the tongue in a forward position, repositioning the lower jaw slightly forward, and increasing the vertical dimension between the upper and lower jaws. Clinical trials have shown the device to be functional, comfortable, and effective.



Tooth Positioner

The tooth positioner developed by Dr. Harold D. Kesling in 1942 was the first custom-made appliance designed to make final adjustments to teeth after orthodontic treatment. It paved the way for modern clear aligners like Invisalign, which are custom-made from a thermoplastic material and can treat a wide range of orthodontic issues over an extended period. Clear aligners focus on individual tooth movement, while tooth positioners use occlusion as the core to facilitate tooth movement.
